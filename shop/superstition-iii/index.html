About this artwork

Superstition III continues the exploration of animal superstition and the deep bonds we share with dogs. This piece delves into the deep-rooted aspects of our relationship with one of the most loyal companions - labradors. Capturing their sweet and playful nature through dramatic contrasts and rich textures.

As part of the Superstition series, this work examines how dogs have been woven into our folklore, superstitions, and daily lives. The limited edition of 100 prints ensures exclusivity while making this piece accessible to collectors and dog lovers alike.
